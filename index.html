St. Augustine's Collect

They that be wise shall shine as the brightness of the firmament; and they that turn many to righteousness as the stars for ever and ever.

V. Blessed is the man whom thou chastenest, O Lord.

R. And teachest him in thy law.

Assist us, Almighty God, in these our supplications: and since thou dost give us confidence in the hope of thy goodness; graciously deign to grant us, at the intercession of blessed Augustine thy Confessor and Bishop, the fruit of thy wonted mercy. Through Jesus Christ our Lord. Amen.
